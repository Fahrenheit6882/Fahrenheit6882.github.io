WHAT IS FAHRENHEIT ROBOTICS? Fahrenheit robotics is a small locally based community with the mission to inspire and encourage young innovators. We are a FIRST robotics team, but WHAT IS FIRST? FIRST is an engineering based organization with the mission to encourage students in engineering and technological fields, FIRST holds many programs and day camps all around the country. How can you contact us? Though we mainly use Discord, we have Gmail and Instagram so feel free to contact us if you ever a question. When are the meetings? During Build Season (January and February) teams attend the kick off event the first saturday after New Years
Who is Wedgewood? Wedgewood is the mascot for Fahrenheit Robotics and our favorite helpful robot!
